Yubo Ma is currently a second-year Ph.D. student from [School of Computer Science and Engineering(SCSE)](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/scse) in [Nanyang Technological University](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/) co-supervised by [Prof. Aixin Sun](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/axsun/) and [Prof. Yixin Cao](https://sites.google.com/view/yixin-homepage). Previously he received B.Eng. degree in [Electronic Engineering](https://www.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/en) from [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) in 2020. He also had interships at [SenseTime Research](https://www.sensetime.com/en). His research interests lie on event-centric information extraction, large language models and knowledge graph.
